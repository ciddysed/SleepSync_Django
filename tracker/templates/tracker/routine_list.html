Relaxation Tips

Morning Relaxation (6:00 AM - 11:00 AM)
  • Start with gentle stretching exercises
  • Practice deep breathing for 5 minutes
  • Meditate in natural morning light
  • Listen to calming morning music
Evening Relaxation (5:00 PM - 8:00 PM)
  • Take a warm bath or shower
  • Do light yoga or stretching
  • Practice mindful breathing
  • Listen to calming music
Night Relaxation (8:00 PM - 10:00 PM)
  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Gentle bedtime stretches
  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Listen to sleep sounds or white noise
